Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is essentially a meat sandwhich without the bread. Italian in nature, it has been known as a stable of many Italian homes.

It consists mainly of pasta, meat, cheese, and sauce in various sections.

Below you will find the ingredients necessary:

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the Noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe
  5. Spices and Seasonings
  6. Cover with foil and bake
  7. Let the lasagna rest before serving
