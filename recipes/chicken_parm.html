Chicken Parmesan

Description

Chicken Parmesan is by-far my favorite dish. From the delictable sauce, to the cheese, to the cooked to perfection chicken. Everything about it is perfect in almost every way.

It consists mainly of chicken, cheese, and marinara sauce in various sections.

Below you will find the ingredients necessary:

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the Noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe
  5. Spices and Seasonings
  6. Cover with foil and bake
  7. Let the lasagna rest before serving
