Chicken Parmesan

Description

Chicken Parmesan is by-far my favorite dish. From the delictable sauce, to the cheese, to the cooked to perfection chicken. Everything about it is perfect in almost every way.

It consists mainly of chicken, cheese, and marinara sauce in various sections.

Below you will find the ingredients necessary:

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup of panko bread crumbs
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbspn flour
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup tomoato sauce
  • 1/4 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1/4 cup basil
  • 1/2 cup grated provolone cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 Celsius)
  2. Place chicken between two sheets of plastic and firmly pound chicken to a thickness of 1/2 inch.
  3. Season chicken thoroughly with salt and pepper
  4. Beat eggs in a shallow bowl
  5. Mix bread crumbs and 1/2 cup of Parmesan in a seperate bowl
  6. Dip flour-coated chicken breast in beaten eggs and let rest for 1- to 15 minutes
  7. Heat 1/2 inch of olive oil in a large skillet on medium-high heat until it shimmers. Cook chicken about 2 minutes per side.
  8. Transfer chicken to a baking dish and top with 2 tbspns of tomato sauce. Layer with mozzarella, basil and provolone.
  9. Head 1/2 inch of olive oil in a large skillet on medium-high heat until it shimmers. Cook chicken about 2 minutes per side.
  10. Bake until cheese is browned and bubbly and chicken breasts are no longer pink in the center (usually 15 to 20 minutes).
  11. Enjoy!
