Sugar Cookies

Description

With dinner out of the way, let's focus on desert! These cookies are simple and delicious and guaranteed to hit the sweet spot!

Below you will find the ingredients necessary:

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Leaveners
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients in one bowl and the wet ingredients in the other.
  2. Gradually blend the flour mixture into the butter mixture.
  3. Roll the dough into balls and bake until the edges are golden.
